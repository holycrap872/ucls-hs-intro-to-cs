    - Who has used Scratch before?
    - Who has used Python before?
- What words do you remember from programming?
    - if
    - repeat
    - variable...

    - ChatGPT:
        - https://github.com/tensorflow/gnn

        - https://freefishy.org/
        - `object_fishy.py`
